Refund Policy for pet-care - Online Pet Adoption

Effective Date: 24-11-2023

Thank you for using pet-care. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds are provided for transactions related to pet adoption on our platform.

1. Adoption Fees

 1.1 Non-Refundable Fees:
Adoption fees paid through our platform are generally non-refundable. These fees contribute to the operational costs of our website and the support of pet adoption services.
1.2 Exceptional Circumstances:
Refunds may be considered in exceptional circumstances, such as a documented error in the transaction amount or other situations deemed appropriate by pet-care.

2. Transaction Errors

 2.1 Incorrect Adoption Fee:
If there is a discrepancy between the advertised adoption fee and the amount charged, pet-care will refund the difference.
2.2 Technical Errors:
In the case of technical errors resulting in overcharging or duplicate charges,pet-care will promptly investigate and refund the excess amount.

3. Pet Health Guarantee

 3.1 Health Issues:
In the event that a pet is adopted, and a significant health issue is discovered within a specified period after adoption, pet-care may consider a refund or alternative resolution. Documentation from a licensed veterinarian is required.

4. Refund Process

 4.1 Refund Request:
To request a refund, users must contact pet-care within a reasonable time frame, providing details of the transaction and the reason for the refund request.
4.2 Refund Evaluation:
Refund requests will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis. pet-care reserves the right to deny refunds for reasons not explicitly outlined in this policy.
4.3 Refund Method:
Refunds, if approved, will be processed using the same method as the original payment.

5. Contact Us

 If you have any questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us at pet-care.
